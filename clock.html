About

Clock is my first Javascript Project, it is inspired by the qlocktwo watch. The goal of this project was to view myself as a potential customer. I tried to replicate every functionality of the watch and wanted a page where the customer could change the time, the background, and could also use the stopwatch setting.

The challenges

Before taking on this project, I was finding it really difficult to grasps functions. I wanted to build a project that was challenging enough and enabled me to practice and understand functions, but most importantly to really start to understand the fundamentals of javascript before diving into a new library.
